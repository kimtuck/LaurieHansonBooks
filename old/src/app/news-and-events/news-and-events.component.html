News and Events

Laurie visited Ramsey School in June, reading her book to 9 elementary school classrooms. A grant from the Ramsey Lions allowed her to donate a copy of Treasure's Gift to each classroom.

She was at the Canine Carnival in April. This is at the Wildlife Science Center in Forest Lake, MN. We got to meet a lot of dog owners and many of them went home with Treasure's Gift.

We will be having a "Christmas in July" sale -- look for it!
