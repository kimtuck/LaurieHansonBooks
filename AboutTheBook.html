About Treasure's Gift

Treasure's Gift was produced and printed by Beaver's Pond Press, a publisher with more than 900 titles designed, produced and printed.

The book is professional quality, with a hard cover and thick, sturdy pages. The pictures on each page were professionally designed and painted by the illustrator, Darcy Bell-Meyer. The book is a chapter book, and 38 pages long.
