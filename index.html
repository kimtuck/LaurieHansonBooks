Laurie announces her new book "Treasure's Gift"

A moving story about a Golden Retriever and her best friend.

Treasure and her best friend, Annie, get some sad news -- Annie's family is moving. Annie is worried that she won't have any friends in the new neighborhood, and her birthday is only two weeks away! But Treasure has a plan. Can Treasure help Annie make new friends?
Treasure's Gift is on sale:
  • at the Red Balloon Bookstore in St. Paul, Minnesota
  • on Amazon.com;
  • through her own website, LaurieHansonBooks.com.
Laurie will also sell her book at the St Paul Kennel Club dog show the weekend of Jan 6-8.
Treasure's Gift is at the Anoka County Library and St. Paul Library!
