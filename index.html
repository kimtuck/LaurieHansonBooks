Treasure's Gift

Laurie announces her new book "Treasure's Gift"

A moving story about a Golden Retriever and her best friend.

Treausure and her best friend, Annie, get some sad news -- Annie's family is moving. Annie is worried that she won't have any friends in the new neighborhood, and her birthday is only two weeks away! But Treasure has a plan. Can Treasure help Annie make new friends?
